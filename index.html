About Us

The Mars Hostel is proudly borne by a growing collection of distinguished properties around the world. As Europe’s oldest luxury hostel group, we are committed to providing our guests with memorable journeys inspired by exquisite European flair. We believe life should be lived with style.

Each year, an increasing number of guests come to appreciate these qualities, as Mars Hostel adds new, exciting destinations in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia and The Americas. While this growth reflects the strength and success of the Mars Hostel brand, the collection will remain a limited one, where exclusivity can be nurtured and individuality can flourish.

We believe that our prestigious, European heritage puts Mars Hostel in a unique position to satisfy the expectations of the stylish and discerning traveller. It’s not just to simply provide a hostel bed and a meal, at Mars Hostel it’s all about bringing a story to life.

Angelina Ospado

Lima, Peru

I really like this hostel. Yes, the male dorm is big, but rarely full. Wifi strong, and great, fully fitted kitchen. Staff not always at hand, but helpful once available. Toilets and showers very clean. Short, pleasant walk into town. Not a party hostel, with many families. That’s fine, given the beauty of the surrounding countryside. Each person gets a key to dorm, and free key to bedside locker. Bring own towels. 24 hour access via door keypad. If arriving late, ring ahead.
